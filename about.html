ABOUT

Neuroalgebra is a collaborative project led by the UCL Laboratory of Synaptic Imaging. The dedicated team behind this initiative includes:

  • Leonid P. Savtchenko
  • Dmitri A. Rusakov

and our long-term industrial collaborators at:

  • AMC Bridge LLC (Waltham MA, USA)
  • Unboltsoft (Dnipro, Ukraine)
  • CyberCurio LLP (Berkhamsted, UK)

To download and explore our computational Tools, please register at Neuroalgebra Forum. For support and suggestions, please consult our Support Page.
